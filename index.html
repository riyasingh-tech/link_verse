Help Centre

Frequently Asked Questions and Support

How do I reset my password?
Go to the login screen and click on "Forgot Password". You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.
Can I change my account type after registration?
Yes, you can update your account type in the profile settings. Some features may require verification for certain account types.
How do I join a study group?
Navigate to the Study Groups section, browse available groups by interest or semester, and click "Join Group" on any group you're interested in.
Is there a limit to how many materials I can upload?
Currently, there's a limit of 10 active uploads per user. You can remove older uploads to make space for new ones.
How do I contact support?
You can reach our support team at support@linkverse.edu or use the chatbot in the bottom right corner of the screen.
